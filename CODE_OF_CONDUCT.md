# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Sky Movie to be a welcoming, respectful open-source project. Everyone
who participates is expected to help create a friendly and constructive
environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent while staying open to feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give and receive criticism constructively.
- Respect differing levels of experience and availability.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith participation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make the project unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or limit
participation when behavior violates this code of conduct. Enforcement should
be proportional, consistent, and focused on protecting the project community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers
through the project's preferred private contact channel. Reports will be
handled with care and confidentiality where possible.
