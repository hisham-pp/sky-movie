# Support

Sky Movie is maintained as an open-source project. Support is community-driven
and depends on maintainer and contributor availability.

## Where to Get Help

- Use issues for reproducible bugs and well-scoped feature requests.
- Use discussions, if enabled, for questions, ideas, and general help.
- Check existing issues before opening a new one.

## Good Support Requests

Helpful requests include:

- A clear description of the problem.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Your operating system, browser, and app version when relevant.
- Screenshots, logs, or console output when they clarify the issue.
- What you have already tried.

## What May Be Out of Scope

Maintainers may not be able to provide:

- Private one-on-one debugging.
- Support for heavily modified forks.
- Help with unrelated infrastructure or third-party services.
- Guaranteed response times.

Clear, reproducible reports are the best way to help the community help you.
